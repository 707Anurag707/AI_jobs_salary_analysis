# AI_jobs_salary_analysis
1.Salary varies with experience level and not job title and employment type. 

2.Variations that are plotted:

-Heatmap: Experience Level vs Remote Ratio

-Boxplot: Salary vs Experience Level 

-Boxplot: Salary vs Employment Type

-Bar chart: Average Salary by Experience Level

-Bar chart: Top 10 Highest Paying Job Title


3.Major Highlights:

-In case of remoteness,Executive levels have highest number and more chances of getting remote jobs

-Executive have highest median salary,variations in salary and most numbers of outliers

-Executive level employee have dispropotionally high mean income when compared with other levels. Avg. or mean income stabilises when we move down.

-job_title and employment type(eg.part time, full time, contractual, freelancing) doesn't impact avg. salary.
